Hi everyone!

I'm an MD-PhD student at Harvard and MIT, studying on the Health Sciences & Technology (HST) Track. I'm interested in genomics, machine learning, and drug discovery in biotech.

Previously, I did my Master's in Management Science at Tsinghua University as a Schwarzman Scholar. And before that, I majored in Biology and Computer Science at Duke, where I studied GPCR signaling mechanisms at the Rajagopal Lab and applications of machine learning in health with the Duke Crucible. Outside of academics, I've completed fellowships at 5AM Ventures and Scale Asia Ventures. I'm currently working as a Data Scientist at Beacon Biosignals.

If you have any shared interests, feel free to shoot me an e-mail!
